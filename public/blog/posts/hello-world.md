# Hello World

Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to start writing for a while now, and here we are.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Engineering** — things I learn while building software, interesting patterns, and deep dives into tech I use day to day.
- **Photography** — the stories behind my photos and my journey learning the craft.
- **Projects** — breakdowns of side projects and the decisions that went into building them.

## Why a self-hosted blog?

I considered Medium and other platforms, but there's something satisfying about owning your own content. This blog is built with plain Markdown files rendered with `react-markdown` — simple, fast, and fully under my control.

Stay tuned for more posts. Thanks for reading!
